Frequently Asked Questions

Below are common questions and answers:

  • Can I access CMS using my institution email directly?

    No, you need to register on the "Get Started" page before accessing CMS.

  • After registering, will I be mapped to my college details?

    No, your university has to be registered in CMS by providing all the necessary details.

  • Can my university register new mappings to CMS?

    No, your university needs to contact our CMS admin to add new mappings.

  • Can I edit my counseling details?

    No, edit access is granted to the university and admin only.
